More Information

The closest airports to Hvar are Split (good connections to Switzerland), Zadar (2h by car), Dubrovnik (3h) and Zagreb (4h).

As it is an island, you can either take the ferry if you have a car, or the catamaran (faster).
Both leave from Split, the ferry docks in Stari Grad while the catamaran goes straight to Grad Hvar.
We recommend booking at least a day in advance, as it can get crowded.

If you come by catamaran, you won't need a car, but if you want the freedom there are options to rent cars, as well as taxis.

October is offseason for Hvar, so accomadation shouldn't be a problem.
There are many Airbnb's for rent, we compiled a list of options here, but there are others as well.

Hotels are on the pricier side, but we suggest Hotel Podstine Hvar, Pharos hotel,
Hotel Amfora, Hotel Sirena, or Labranda Senses Resort (not in Hvar, but nice and cheap) if you prefer that option.
